📚 What are Modal Verbs?

Modal verbs express ability, permission, obligation, possibility, probability, and more. They are auxiliary verbs that modify the main verb.

Key Rules: Modal + base verb (no "to"), no -s/-ed/-ing forms.

✅ Ability & Permission
  • can swim
  • could help
  • may leave
  • might come
  • shall begin
⚖️ Obligation & Advice
  • must study
  • should call
  • ought to apologize
  • need to finish
  • have to attend
🎲 Possibility & Probability
  • will rain
  • would help
  • may work
  • might fail
  • could happen
