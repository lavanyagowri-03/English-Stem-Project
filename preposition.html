📚 What are Prepositions?

Prepositions are words that show the relationship between a noun/pronoun and other words in a sentence. They indicate place, time, direction, manner, agent, measure, and more.

Key Point: Prepositions are always followed by a noun or pronoun (object of the preposition).

🏠 Place Prepositions
  • in the room
  • on the table
  • at the door
  • under the bed
  • beside the chair
⏰ Time Prepositions
  • at 5 PM
  • on Monday
  • in July
  • before lunch
  • after school
➡️ Direction Prepositions
  • to the store
  • into the house
  • through the tunnel
  • across the street
  • over the bridge
